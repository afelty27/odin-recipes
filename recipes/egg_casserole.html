Egg Casserole

Description

This egg casserole is a delicious take on an old classic. It combines a simple breakfast dish of eggs with a scrumptious combination of cheese, veggies, and sausage. This will become a family classic!

Ingredients

  • 12 eggs
  • 1lb breakfast sausage
  • 1 medium onion
  • Two bell peppers
  • 6oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 6oz heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Begin to saute onion and bell peppers over high heat in skillet
  3. When halfway cooked, add breakfast sausage
  4. About three minutes before sausage is fully brown, add diced garlic cloves
  5. Beat eggs in a separate mixing bowl. Then, add the saute mixture to the bowl along with the cheese and whipping cream. Stir until well mixed
  6. Pour mixture into casserole dish and bake for 40min or until center is fully cooked
