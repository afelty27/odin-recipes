Enchilada Skillet

Description

This is a fantastically easy, yet delicious way to cook your favorite enchilada recipe. And to top it all off, it only requires one pan!

Ingredients

>
  • 1lb ground turkey
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 can corn
  • 12oz enchilada suace
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 6oz shredded cheddar
  • 6 tortillas

Steps

  1. Dice bell peppers, onion, and garlic
  2. Saute bell peppers, onion, and on high heat
  3. When halfway done, add turkey sausage. With 3 minutes left until sausage cooked, add garlic
  4. Add enchilada suace, both beans, and corn. Heat thoroughly
  5. Rip up and add tortillas. Add cheese. Heat until cheese is melted and serve
